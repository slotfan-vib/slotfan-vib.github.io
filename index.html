Saeed Lotfan

Faculty Member at Gebze Technical University
Department of Mechanical Engineering

slotfan@gtu.edu.tr

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Gebze Technical University. I earned my B.S., M.S., and Ph.D. degrees in Mechanical Engineering at University of Tabriz, Tabriz, Iran in 2012, 2014, and 2018, respectively. I later joined Sabanci University in Istanbul, Turkey as a postdoctoral researcher.

My primary research interests lie in the field of mechanical vibrations, encompassing both linear and nonlinear systems. I focus on developing advanced mathematical models and efficient numerical techniques to accurately capture the complex dynamics of structures. My work also extends to signal processing for inverse problems and structural health monitoring, as well as computational mechanics for high-fidelity simulations and reduced-order modeling.
