Research Projects

Design of Stiffened Composite Panels

TÜBİTAK 1001 (2023–2026)

PI: Saeed Lotfan

Partner: Istanbul Technical University

Stiffened laminated panels are widely used in aerospace, marine, and automotive applications due to their high stiffness-to-weight ratio. This project develops a novel spectral element methodology to accurately model and optimize the structural behavior of stiffened composite panels. The method incorporates NURBS-based mesh generation for geometric precision, allowing fewer elements while maintaining high accuracy. This significantly reduces simulation time compared to conventional finite element approaches.
