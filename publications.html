Abstract: This study proposes an artificial neural network (ANN)-based model integrated with a non-dominated sorting genetic algorithm II (NSGA-II) to minimize emissions in a dual-fuel engine. The model is trained on experimental data and used to optimize operational parameters. The results demonstrate significant reductions in NOx and CO emissions without compromising engine performance, validating the effectiveness of ANN-NSGA-II frameworks in energy systems.
